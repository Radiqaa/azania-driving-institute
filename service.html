Azania Driving Institute

ADI offers a comprehensive driver training program designed to equip students with the skills and knowledge necessary to become safe and responsible drivers. Our program consists of three key phases:

Our Services

📱

Free Learners Course

Duration: 5 weeks
Content: Traffic laws, road signs, vehicle controls, tire changing, risk assessment, and learner's license test preparation.
Delivery: Certified trainers conduct in-person lectures at the college venue.
Benefits: Prepares students for the learner's license test and facilitates group bookings for the test.

💻

K53 Simulator Driving Phase (Paid)

Duration: 10 lessons per student
Content: Practical driving skills and road rules in a safe, simulated environment.
Delivery: ADI's state-of-the-art driving simulators.
Benefits: Minimizes risk while practicing driving skills and prepares students for the road-driving phase.
